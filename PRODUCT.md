# Product

## Register

product

## Users

World Cup fans watching with friends, families, communities, or sponsor-led groups. They are usually on a phone during a live match and want to call the result, react together, invite others, and see who was right without learning crypto mechanics or funding a wallet.

## Product Purpose

Fan Zone turns TxLINE's live odds and scores into a shared, provably-fair sweepstake room. A fan signs in through Solana, locks a prediction, watches the consensus probability move in real time, follows the room's reactions and leaderboard, and sees the final result verified from TxLINE's proof. Success means a new fan can join, understand the match state, make a call, and trust the outcome in under a minute.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, trustworthy, communal. The product should feel like a premium live sports broadcast made interactive: confident enough for technical proof, lively enough for a watch party, and plain-spoken enough for a first-time Solana user.

## Anti-references

- Casino and sportsbook interfaces that use urgency, dark patterns, or financialized language as the primary experience.
- Generic crypto dashboards that lead with wallet jargon, balances, token tickers, or developer infrastructure.
- Fake-live products that animate placeholders or simulated probabilities without labeling them.
- Toy-like fan apps built from emoji, confetti everywhere, novelty gradients, or interchangeable card grids.
- Interfaces that copy another submission's identity, content, deployment URL, or README structure.

## Design Principles

1. **The live match is the product.** Every important interaction stays anchored to the real fixture, score, probability, and update provenance.
2. **Social before financial.** The primary reward is group status, conversation, and bragging rights; monetization belongs to rooms and sponsors, not exploitative wagering mechanics.
3. **Proof should reduce anxiety.** Solana commitments and TxLINE proofs are presented as calm, human-readable receipts with deeper details available on demand.
4. **Make degraded states honest.** Live, replay, cached, scheduled, and unavailable data are visibly distinct; Fan Zone never fabricates activity.
5. **Wallet-first without wallet friction.** Solana signup establishes ownership at the front door, while embedded-wallet and keyless judge paths keep onboarding accessible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. All core flows must work by keyboard and on a 390px-wide phone; controls use at least 44px touch targets. Score, pick, and connection states cannot rely on color alone. Text and placeholders meet 4.5:1 contrast where required. Motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`, and live updates use non-disruptive announcements rather than stealing focus.
